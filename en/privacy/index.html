1. Scope

This Privacy Policy ("Policy") applies to the iOS application "ShohoNote" ("App") and any pages on our website that link to this Policy (collectively, "Service"). By downloading, installing, or using the App, you agree to this Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information you provide

  • Email address — for account registration and login authentication.
  • Nickname — as a display name in community features. You are not required to enter your real name.
  • Password — stored hashed in the Supabase authentication backend; the plaintext is never accessible to us.
  • Community content — review text, ratings (4 axes), tags, and other content you submit through community features. This content is publicly viewable to other users of the Service.
  • Medication history data — the medication log you enter for yourself and your registered family members (drug name, date/time, dosage, notes). This data is not public and is used only by you and your registered family members.
  • Family member information — display names for the family members you register. We recommend a non-real-name string.

2.2 Information collected automatically

  • User ID — an internal identifier issued by Supabase; used only within the Service for account identification.
  • File Timestamp — Apple Required-Reason-API category C617.1; used by SwiftData and FileManager internals.
  • System Boot Time — Apple Required-Reason-API category 35F9.1; used by MetricKit for performance telemetry and cache freshness arithmetic.
  • UserDefaults — Apple Required-Reason-API category CA92.1; used for app state preservation.

Note (added per Phase 216 audit): among the items above, "nickname", "medication history", and "family member display names" are declared under Apple's App Privacy category NSPrivacyCollectedDataTypeOtherUserContent (user-generated content) in PrivacyInfo.xcprivacy. Because ShohoNote does not integrate with HealthKit, medication history is classified as User Content rather than Health and Fitness data.

2.3 Information we do not collect

  • Advertising identifier (IDFA) — no ad SDK is integrated (NSPrivacyTracking = false).
  • Location data — the App does not use any location APIs.
  • HealthKit data — the App does not integrate with HealthKit.
  • Contacts / Photos / Camera / Microphone — none of these are accessed.
  • Third-party analytics or tracking SDKs (Google Analytics, Firebase, Sentry, Crashlytics, etc.) — none are integrated.
