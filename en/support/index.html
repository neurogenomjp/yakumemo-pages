ShohoNote — Support

NOTICE — This document is an autopilot-generated draft. Please review before publishing.

Thank you for using ShohoNote. Below are answers to frequently asked questions and how to contact us.

Contact

  • Email: tmedilab.support@gmail.com
  • In-app entry point: tap Settings → Terms & Policies → Contact; your browser opens this page (wired in Phase 212 / 2026-06-16).

We typically reply within 2–5 business days. Inquiries received on weekends or Japanese national holidays are handled on the next business day.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. I paid but Premium features are still locked

Please try the following:

  1. In the app, tap "My Page → Restore Premium".
  2. Open iOS Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions and confirm that "Yakumemo Premium" is active.
  3. If the above does not resolve the issue, email tmedilab.support@gmail.com with your Apple ID email and purchase date/time.

Q2. How do I cancel my subscription

Manage and cancel subscriptions from iOS Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions, or via:

  • https://apps.apple.com/account/subscriptions

We are unable to cancel Apple-managed subscriptions on your behalf.

Q3. Why can't I add more family members

Free-plan accounts have a cap on family-member registration. Subscribing to Premium raises the cap.

The maximum number of family members is 5.

Q4. PDF export isn't working

PDF export is a Premium-only feature and is not available on the Free plan.

Q5. Drug information looks outdated or incorrect

The drug master is compiled from publicly available data published by Japan's Pharmaceuticals and Medical Devices Agency (PMDA). If you notice an inaccuracy or stale entry, please email the drug name and details to tmedilab.support@gmail.com.

Please remember that ShohoNote does not replace guidance from healthcare professionals. Always consult a physician or pharmacist about your actual medication regimen.

Q6. I want to report an inappropriate review

Tap "Report" on the review in question. We will review the report and act promptly if the content violates our Terms of Service.

To hide a specific user's posts, use the "Block" feature.

Q7. How do I delete my account

In the app, go to "My Page → Account Deletion". After deletion, all related data we manage is deleted within 30 days.

Q8. I forgot my password

On the login screen, tap "Forgot password?" to receive a password-reset email at your registered address.
